About Us

At Noble Crest Horses, we are passionate about breeding and caring for the finest Quarter and Friesian horses. Our mission is to connect horse lovers with healthy, well-trained, and majestic horses that bring joy, companionship, and unmatched performance.

With years of experience, we pride ourselves on trust, quality, and dedication. Every horse we raise is given the best care, ensuring they are strong, elegant, and ready for their next home.

Shipping Information

Delivery of Horses

For horse purchases, please contact us directly to discuss transportation options and arrangements. We partner with certified and reputable horse transporters to ensure safe and comfortable transit.

Delivery fees for horses are calculated separately based on distance, horse handling requirements, and transporter availability.

We also assist with door-to-door delivery, export documentation, and international shipping upon request.
